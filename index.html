Top attractions in Berlin

Brandenburg Gate

The Brandenburg Gate was once a city gate into Berlin. Nowadays, it is one of the most important symbols of Berlin and an emblem of peace.

East Side Gallery

Berlin is a city that never forgets its recent past. The remaining stretches of wall are now a lasting part of Berlin and the East Side Gallery is one of the most memorable landmarks in Berlin. A definite must-see.

Charlottenburg Palace

One of the most charming areas of Charlottenburg are the extensive and well cared for gardens designed in 1697 in a French Baroque style and reformed in an English romantic style in 1788.
